Welcome to **Career Edge**, the all-in-one platform designed to revolutionize the hiring process for both job seekers and companies. Powered by AI, Career Edge streamlines everything from job applications to candidate management, making it easier than ever to connect talent with opportunity.

## ‚ú® Core Features

Career Edge is built with distinct user roles, each with a tailored set of powerful features.

### For Candidates

-   **AI-Powered Job Matching**: Discover roles that perfectly match your skills and ambitions.
-   **Seamless Application Process**: Apply to jobs with a streamlined, user-friendly interface.
-   **Application Tracking**: Monitor the status of all your job applications from a central dashboard.
-   **AI Resume Analysis**: Get instant AI feedback on how well your resume aligns with a specific job description to optimize your chances.
-   **Online Assessments**: Complete screening and technical assessments directly on the platform.
-   **Personalized Dashboard**: View your application history and upcoming interview schedules at a glance.

### For Companies & Recruiters

-   **Intuitive Job Posting**: Easily create and manage job postings.
-   **Applicant Tracking System (ATS)**: Manage your entire hiring pipeline, from application to offer, with a visual Kanban board or a detailed list view.
-   **AI Resume Screening**: Automatically screen and score resumes against your job requirements to instantly identify top candidates.
- a   **Custom Assessments**: Build and send custom skills assessments to objectively evaluate candidate abilities.
-   **Company & Custom Question Banks**: Create and manage a library of interview questions to ensure a consistent and effective interview process.
-   **Team Management**: Invite and manage roles for different account managers (Admin, Editor, Viewer).

### For Platform Administrators

-   **User Management**: Oversee all candidate and company accounts, with tools to manage their status and plans.
-   **Subscription & Coupon Management**: Configure and manage subscription plans for both candidates and companies, and create promotional offers.
-   **Content Management**: Update platform-wide content such as "What's New," "About Us," and legal policies directly through the dashboard.
-   **Platform Analytics**: A high-level overview of user growth and platform activity.
